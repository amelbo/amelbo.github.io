About Me

Hi there! I am a doctoral candidate at the Aalto Human Computer Interaction and Security Engineering Lab, Department of Computer Science, Aalto University. My research interests are focused on investigating the usability of Cloud services and user awareness of privacy, understanding cognitive processes involved with making privacy and security related choices on different platforms, and implementing solutions to assist users in making better decisions.

On this website you will find relevant blog posts to my research journey that I hope you can find helpful to your own research. I will be adding more content later on such as publications and other projects.

Please feel free to drop me a message on LinkedIn. You can find my other social media at the bottom of this webpage.
